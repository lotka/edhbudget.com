Why did you build this?

A budget is a great way to keep EDH casual and accessible, however it's really hard to measure how much a deck costs, some cards have many different editions and prices change everyday, this tool allows you to get a stable price which only changes if you include the latest cards.

How are card prices calculated?

The price of a card is the average of cheapest edition of a card on each day.

What is a season?

Prices are calcuated on a season basis, at the moment there are 3 seasons:
  • S1 - January, February, March, April
  • S2 - May, June, July, August
  • S3 - September, October, November, December

    • What if a card doesn't exist in the last seasons?

    Then the newer season is used and the card prices will fluctuate
